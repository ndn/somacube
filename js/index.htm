The soma cube is an interesting puzzle. You can learn about it here:

  • http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Soma_cube

I thought it would be fun to write an algorithm that solves the problems on the fifty cards that came with the soma cube I have. Somehow things didn't stop there and this website is the result of a couple of weeks spent obsessing over this thing.

Both the algorithm (in python) and the contents on this website can be found on github under the MIT license:

  • https://github.com/ndn/somacube

As you might notice this website has much more than fifty problems by now. The first 35 problems are taken from the "Piet Hein SOMA booklet" written by the inventor of the soma cube. All other problems are here thanks to and with kind permission of Thorleif Bundgaard, who has collected and invented several hundred soma cube problems on his website. I strongly suggest you to check it out:

  • http://www.fam-bundgaard.dk/SOMA/SOMA.HTM

All solutions to the problems on this website were computed by my own algorithm.
